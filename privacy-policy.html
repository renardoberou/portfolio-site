Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026. This policy covers the Resonant Systems Android apps published by Bernardo Roustand (Resonant Systems), including The Bighart.

Summary

These apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. They do not contain analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking of any kind. Each app is a standalone, offline instrument — it does not request the Internet permission and cannot send information anywhere, because it has no network access at all.

What data is collected

None. No account, no sign-in, no personal information, no device identifiers, no location, no contacts, no camera/microphone access beyond what's needed for the app's own on-device audio engine to function, and no usage analytics are collected by the app or by the developer.

What is stored, and where

Some apps let you save settings or presets (for example, synthesizer patches). This data is stored only locally on your device, using standard on-device storage (such as localStorage within the app's embedded web view). It is never uploaded, synced, or made accessible to the developer or to any third party. Uninstalling the app removes this data along with it.

Permissions

These apps request only the Android permissions strictly required for their core function (for example, audio playback). None of the apps request the Internet permission unless a specific feature explicitly requires network access, in which case that feature and its data use will be described in that app's own listing and, if applicable, in an update to this policy.

Third parties

No data is shared with any third party, because no data leaves your device. There are no third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or crash reporting bundled in these apps.

Children's privacy

These apps do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because they do not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of an app changes its data practices, this page will be updated first, with a new "last updated" date above, before that version is published.

Contact
