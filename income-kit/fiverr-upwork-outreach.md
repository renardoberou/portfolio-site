## Fiverr Gig 1 — Business AI Workflow and Automation

### Gig title
I will design practical AI workflows and automation for your business

### Category
Programming & Tech / AI Development, or Business / Business Consulting if Fiverr suggests it.

### Search tags
AI automation, AI consultant, business automation, prompt engineering, workflow audit

### Short gig description
I will review your business process and design a practical AI workflow that reduces manual work, improves research/reporting, and keeps human review where it matters.

### Package structure

Basic — AI Opportunity Scan — $25
- Review one workflow or business process
- Identify 3–5 AI opportunities
- Recommend tools and first actions
- 1-page summary
- Delivery: 2 days

Standard — AI Workflow Blueprint — $95
- Review up to three workflows
- Map inputs, outputs, tools and human-review points
- Provide a step-by-step implementation plan
- Include prompt templates and operating notes
- Delivery: 4 days

Premium — AI Implementation Plan — $250
- Full workflow blueprint for one department or recurring business process
- Tool/API/agent recommendations
- Prompt library and SOP draft
- Risk notes and data-handling recommendations
- 30-minute walkthrough call
- Delivery: 7 days

### Full description
Most businesses are being told to “use AI” without being shown where it actually helps. I provide practical AI workflow design: I look at your current process, identify where AI can reduce manual work, and define a safe, usable system that your team can understand.

This gig is useful for small businesses, agencies, consultants, clinics, hospitality teams, creators and operational teams that need better research, reporting, documentation, client communication, internal knowledge retrieval or repetitive admin support.

I can help with:
- AI opportunity audits
- Workflow and process mapping
- Tool selection
- Prompt libraries
- Research and reporting workflows
- Internal knowledge-assistant concepts
- Lightweight agent-system plans
- Automation roadmaps
- Human-review and quality-control design

I do not sell hype. The goal is a clear plan you can act on.

### Buyer requirements
1. Briefly describe your business.
2. Describe the workflow or task you want to improve.
3. What tools do you currently use? Examples: Gmail, Google Docs, Excel, Notion, WhatsApp, CRM, PDF files, internal documents.
4. What output do you want? Example: faster reports, better customer replies, document summaries, structured research, fewer repetitive tasks.
5. Are there any privacy, legal, medical, financial or security constraints?

### FAQ
Q: Will you build the automation?
A: This gig focuses on audit, design and implementation planning. I can quote separately for prototype builds.

Q: Can you work with non-technical teams?
A: Yes. The deliverables are written for decision-makers and operators, not only developers.

Q: Do you use ChatGPT only?
A: No. I recommend tools based on the workflow. That may include ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, APIs, automation platforms, document search, or custom prototypes.

Q: Can you guarantee cost savings?
A: No. I can identify opportunities, estimate impact and design a practical workflow, but results depend on implementation and adoption.

### Proposal 1 — AI automation / business workflow
Hello, I can help you turn this into a practical AI workflow rather than a vague automation idea.

My approach would be:
1. Map the current process and outputs.
2. Identify which steps are safe to automate and which need human review.
3. Recommend the right tools or model/API setup.
4. Provide prompts, SOPs and an implementation plan your team can actually use.

I work as a business AI consultant and creative technologist, with experience designing structured AI workflows, agent systems, corporate AI training and technical research processes. I’m based in Brazil and work fluently in English and Portuguese.

Relevant portfolio: https://renardoberou.github.io/portfolio-site/

A useful first step would be to review one workflow and define the smallest version that can deliver measurable time savings.

### Proposal 2 — AI consultant / prompt engineering
Hello, this is a good fit for my work. I design prompt systems and AI workflows for real business tasks: research, reporting, document handling, customer-response drafts, content systems and internal knowledge workflows.

I would not start by writing prompts in isolation. I would first clarify:
- What input data the workflow receives
- What output quality is required
- Where human review must remain
- Which model or tool is appropriate
- How the workflow will be repeated by non-technical users

Then I would deliver a reusable prompt/workflow structure, documentation and testing notes.

Portfolio: https://renardoberou.github.io/portfolio-site/
