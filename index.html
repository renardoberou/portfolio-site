About Me

I’m Bernardo Roustand — an independent developer, researcher, and writer. My background crosses software, experimental music, long-form research, symbolic systems, and hands-on product building.

I grew up between languages and cultures, including Switzerland, Brazil, English, Portuguese, and French. That multilingual background shapes how I design interfaces: precise enough to be useful, atmospheric enough to be remembered.

I’m especially interested in the meeting point between sound and interface, atmosphere and usability, research and implementation, AI assistance and durable human judgment.
