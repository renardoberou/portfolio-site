About Me

I’m Bernardo Roustand, an independent developer building browser instruments, Android app prototypes, and AI-assisted workflows. My work sits between software, sound design, research, and product execution.

I grew up between Switzerland and Brazil and work naturally in English, Portuguese, and French. That background makes me attentive to clarity, translation, and context: interfaces should be precise, usable, and still have a distinct atmosphere.

My current focus is Resonant Systems: small, high-character tools for music, imaging, and creative work. I prefer lean products that can be tested on real devices, documented honestly, and improved through concrete feedback.
